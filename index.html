Stack & Blast

Drop blocks. Build groups. Blast them off the board.

A puzzle game with a twist: when same-color blocks form a big enough group, they detonate — and every surrounding block gets pushed outward. One blast triggers another, combos multiply your score, and the board reshuffles with every explosion.

Download on the App Store

Three Ways to Play

♻

Classic

No timer, no pressure. Place pieces, build clusters, and trigger blasts. The threshold rises as your score climbs — how far can you go?

📅

Daily Challenge

60 seconds. Same puzzle for every player, worldwide. The pieces are identical for everyone each day — pure skill.

⚡

Blast Rush

90 seconds on the clock, but every blast adds 5 more. Chain reactions keep the timer alive. Play fast, think ahead.

What Makes It Different

💥

Push Mechanic

Blasts push adjacent blocks outward. Pushed blocks can collide with others, creating chain reactions. Blocks shoved off the edge are gone for good.

🔥

Cascading Combos

Each cascade level doubles your score multiplier. A single blast can trigger a cascade that transforms the entire board.

⭐

Power-Ups

Row Blast, Column Blast, and Color Bomb. Special blocks spawn every 8 pieces — trigger them in a blast to unleash their effect.

Your Board, Your Rules
